Our Story

Let us introduce ourselves: Platinum Plus Catering is owned and operated by Mike and Karina Meyers. We are a married couple; two Chefs with a world- class experience - from- Mexico, to Monaco, to New York, and Florida.

We have an extensive background in hospitality, international hotels, Michelin starred restaurants and high-end catering. Our passion is to work closely with our clients to plan and execute events/parties, anything and everything from dinner for two to a large-scale banquet.
